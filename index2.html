My Country - Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is an island country located in the Indian Ocean, off the southern coast of India. It was formerly known as Ceylon until 1972. Sri Lanka has a rich cultural and historical heritage, with evidence of human settlements dating back to prehistoric times. The country has been ruled by various kingdoms and empires throughout its history, including the Anuradhapura Kingdom, the Polonnaruwa Kingdom, the Kotte Kingdom, and the Kandyan Kingdom.
Sri Lanka gained independence from British colonial rule in 1948 and has since developed into a multi-ethnic and multi-religious society. The majority of the population is Sinhalese, with significant Tamil, Muslim, and other minority groups. The country has a rich biodiversity, with tropical forests, wildlife reserves, and national parks, including the famous Yala National Park, which is home to leopards, elephants, and other wildlife.
Sri Lanka is also known for its beaches, ancient ruins, temples, and cultural festivals, including the famous Esala Perahera festival held annually in the city of Kandy. The country's economy is largely based on agriculture, tourism, and manufacturing.
